Short Review

This paper presents several extensions that improve both the quality of the vectors and the training speed by subsampling, negative sampling, and learning good vector representation from finding phrases in the text.   Original skip-gram model had to calculate all W, which is often large. Rather than doing this, this paper suggest to use hierarchical softmax which uses binary tree representation for words and use sigmoid function to calculate probabilities. This method reduces cost of computing. Also this paper suggest alternative to the hierarchical softmax, NCE(Noise Contrastive Estimation).  This paper define Negative sampling(NEG) by the objective than NCE. This task is to distinguish the target word Wo from draws from the noise distribution, using logistic regression where there are k negative samples for each data sample. Subsampling focus on most frequent words which can easily occur many times. To counter the imbalance between the rare and frequent words, it does subsampling that make a threshold, and if probability is enough bigger and reach the threshold, subsample for this word.  Learning Phrases is finding words that appear frequently together, and infrequently in other contexts. This paper make these words as unique tokens in the training data, and like subsampling, make a threshold and if count score is enough bigger and reach the threshold, these words is one phrase.
